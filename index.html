ABOUT

Bucwengile Construction(Pty) Ltd is a 100% black female, youth owned company. It was establish in 2015 by Sibongile Jas, out of the desire to create sustainable empowerment and participation in the country's economy. One of the Bucwengile Construction's main focus is to empower the previously disadvantage individuals by giving them opportunities to acquire experience so they can ultimately reach their full potential.
Our objective is to provide clients with an "I am guaranteed" encounter when are chosen to execute their respoected projects.

  • We create a detailed schedule and resources plan to meet the client's need
  • We keep track of the project progress
  • We closely supervide the quality of the work done
  • We complte the project on record time
